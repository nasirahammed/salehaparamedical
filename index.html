Welcome to Our Website

Saleha Paramedical College under the management of Saleha Charitable Trust® Kolkata is situated at Airport,Kolkata – 700052, a place well connected by bus routes to all destinations.

Saleha Medical Hospital (previously known as Saleha Nursing Home) the parent institution, established in the year 2000 has now developed as a 250 bedded Multi-specialty Hospital, which caters patients from cross section of the socio-economic stratum by providing latest high-tech equipments handled by Professionals.
